Gilley, the peaceful little mountain lion and friend of The Oswalds loves meditation.

In this Episode he visits a hidden Thai Wat with a beautiful garden in the north of Berlin, Germany.

Follow his tiny pawsteps, find a bench to sit on and listen to the many small bells ringing softly in the wind...

This episode presents another hidden spot in Berlin, this time the Wat Buddhavihara.
